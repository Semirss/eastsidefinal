About Our Vision

At EastSide Agro Industry, our journey is rooted in a deep commitment to quality and sustainability. We are not just producers; we are custodians of a rich agricultural heritage. Our mission is to harness the fertile lands of Ethiopia to bring you products that are not only of the highest quality but also a testament to our ethical and responsible practices.

We believe in a future where agriculture coexists in harmony with the environment, and our operations are designed to reflect this. We are dedicated to empowering local communities and ensuring that every bean of coffee and every spice we process contributes positively to the lives of our farmers and the health of our planet.

Our vision extends beyond borders. We aim to be a global ambassador of Ethiopian produce, sharing the unique flavors and aromas of our land with the world. Join us in this journey of taste and tradition, and experience the true essence of EastSide Agro Industry.

Our Products

We specialize in a variety of premium agricultural products, each cultivated with care and processed to perfection.

Coffee

Our ethically sourced Ethiopian coffee beans are known for their rich aroma and complex flavor profiles. From the highlands to your cup, we ensure a premium experience.

Spices

We offer a range of traditional Ethiopian spices like berbere, mitmita, and shiro, all freshly ground to retain their authentic taste and potency.

Herbs

Our selection of dried herbs, including basil, oregano, and rosemary, are cultivated using organic methods to deliver pure and intense flavors.

Our Process

We follow a meticulous process to ensure every product meets our high standards of quality and sustainability.

1

Sourcing

We partner with local farmers, ensuring fair trade practices and sustainable farming methods.

2

Processing

Our state-of-the-art facilities clean, sort, and process the raw materials while preserving their natural quality.

3

Roasting & Grinding

For our coffee, we apply precise roasting techniques. Spices are freshly ground to your specifications.

4

Packaging & Distribution

We package our products in eco-friendly materials and distribute them to local and international markets.
