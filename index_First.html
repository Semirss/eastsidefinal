Our Heritage & Commitment

Established in 2019 under Ethiopian commercial registration #980/2016, EastSide Agro Industry PLC is a premier agro-processing and export company dedicated to showcasing Ethiopia's finest coffee to the world. We combine traditional cultivation methods with modern quality standards to deliver exceptional Arabica coffee while maintaining ethical business practices.

Our Mission

To connect global coffee lovers with Ethiopia\'s rich coffee heritage through exceptional quality, reliable service, and sustainable partnerships that benefit both farmers and consumers.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia\'s most trusted coffee exporter, recognized worldwide for quality, consistency, and ethical sourcing from the birthplace of Arabica coffee.

Our Values

Unity, Respect, Innovation, Excellence, Integrity, Determination.

These core principles guide every decision we make, from sourcing the finest beans to delivering exceptional customer experiences. We believe that success comes from staying true to these values while continuously evolving and improving our craft.

Our Premium Coffee Selections

Ethiopia is the origin of countless Arabica coffee varieties, each with its own distinctive flavor profile. We work closely with local farmers to process quality coffee with great care and efficiency, honoring the unique characteristics of each growing region.

Yirgachefe

Citrus and floral notes, fine acidity and a full body. Grown at 1770 - 2200m altitude.

Jimma

Hard balanced flavor with a light acidity and a good, heavy body. Grown at 1400 - 1800m altitude.

Sidama

Sweet and spicy flavor, with a perfectly balanced acidity and body. Grown at 1400 - 2200m altitude.

Harar

Distinctive mocha flavor with medium to light acidity. Grown at 1510 - 2120m altitude.

Bebeka

Large, thick beans with light acidity and a medium body. Grown at 950 - 1285m altitude.

Limmu

Well-balanced cup with a distinct wine-like flavor, medium acidity and body. Grown at 1400 - 2100m altitude.

Gujji

Sweet and spicy flavor with balanced acidity and body. Grown at 1400 - 2200m altitude.

Nekemt

A delightful fruit flavor with a medium body and a pointed acidity. Grown at 1770 - 2200m altitude.

Our Roasted Coffee

A Mother's Blessing in Every Cup

Enku Coffee carries a mother's blessing in every cup. Sourced from Yirgacheffe's highlands, this specialty coffee features vibrant citrus, floral notes, and natural sweetness - best enjoyed black to appreciate its authentic Ethiopian character. Roasted at origin and fairly traded, each batch supports sustainable farming while delivering the world\'s finest coffee directly to you.
